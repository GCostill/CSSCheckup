Klaus

When I was first looking for a dog, I was interested in getting a hypoallergenic and calm breed to have as a kind of therapy dog. I came across a pet website and found a dog named Freya that I really wanted. Freya was already adopted, but was part of a rescue center that told me they just rescued a momma that had puppies and they all got adopted except for the runt of the litter. His name was Klaus.

Puppy's Beginning

We decided to get Klaus, and when we went to pick him up and I saw him for the first time, all I could say was "awwww puppy." And since them, I haven't called him anything else but "Puppy."

Crazy Dog

Puppy definitely wasn't anything that we were looking for. He sheds like a husky and has sooo much energy that he's not afraid to tackle you with. He's can be a misbehaving psycho sometimes... but he's my psycho and I love him for it. He's a very smart dog and loves learning and going on adventures just as much as I do! He has so much potential, and I can't wait to help him grow to reach that potential!
